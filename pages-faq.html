Basic Questions
1. What is the purpose of this website?

This website provides detailed information on electricity access and energy consumption across various regions and districts in Ghana. It is designed to help policymakers, researchers, and the public make informed decisions related to energy and sustainable development.

2. How is the data on electricity access collected?

The data presented on this website is sourced from the Ghana Statistical Service (GSS) and various energy agencies. It is collected through national surveys and energy reports, ensuring accuracy and relevance for stakeholders.

3. How can I access Electricity data for my district?

To view energy data for specific districts, simply navigate to the "Energy Consumption by Region" section, click on your region, and scroll down to find detailed district-level electricity access and consumption statistics.
