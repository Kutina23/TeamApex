Electricity Consumption in Ghana /2017-2022
Explanation: . The trend in electricity consumption by the various sectors has been increasing steadily over the years, from 6,889 GWh in 2000 to 17,547 GWh in 2022, representing an annual rate of 4.3%. In terms of sectors, the residential sector has consistently been the largest consumer of electricity, followed by the industrial sector. The service sector has been the third largest consumer of electricity. This indicates that the demand for electricity in the residential sector is high, possibly due to increasing urbanization and population growth.
